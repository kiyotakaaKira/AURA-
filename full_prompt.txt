## SECTION 0 — CONTEXT & PHILOSOPHY

### What AURA Is

AURA is an AI-powered Human Computer Interface (HCI) platform that replaces mouse and keyboard workflows with intelligent, gesture-based, context-aware interaction. The system reads hand position, finger configuration, motion trajectory, facial attention, and user history to predict and execute intent — not just map gestures to actions.

### What AURA Is Not

- Not a virtual mouse replacement
- Not an OpenCV + MediaPipe demo
- Not a college gesture project
- Not a proof-of-concept with hardcoded mappings
- Not a novelty you use once and forget

### The Core Insight

Every gesture system before AURA fails for the same reason: they force the user to adapt to the computer. AURA reverses this. The system adapts to the user. The user should never memorize commands. The system should learn their habits, their motion style, their shortcuts, and their intent.
